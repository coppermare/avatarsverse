# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We strive to provide a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone contributing to Avatarverse.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or discriminatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

## Contact

Report issues via [GitHub security advisories](https://github.com/coppermare/avatarverse/security/advisories/new) or by contacting the maintainers.
